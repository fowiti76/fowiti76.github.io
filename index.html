About Fidel

I Started Speaking
at Fourteen.

I'm Fidel Owiti, public speaker, coach, author, and founder of Speak to Lead. I built this from a single coaching session with no office, no staff, and no capital into a training organisation that has developed hundreds of communicators across Kenya and East Africa.

At 26, I wrote 26 books in one year. I founded the Kenya Association of Public Speakers (KAPS). I coach executives, students, and professionals who have something important to say and need the tools to say it powerfully.

My belief is simple: communication is leadership. The moment you learn to speak with clarity and confidence, everything changes, your influence, your impact, and your income.
