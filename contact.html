Common Questions

Frequently Asked
Questions.

How quickly will I hear back after submitting the form?
Fidel personally reviews every submission and responds within 24-48 hours. For urgent enquiries, WhatsApp is the fastest channel.
Do you offer online coaching sessions?
Yes. Coaching sessions are available both in-person in Nairobi and online via Zoom or Google Meet for clients across Kenya and internationally.
How far in advance should I book Fidel for a speaking engagement?
We recommend reaching out at least 2-4 weeks in advance for corporate events and 1-2 weeks for smaller engagements. Fidel's calendar fills quickly so early booking is advised.
Does Fidel speak outside Nairobi?
Yes. Fidel has spoken across Kenya and is available for engagements across East Africa. Travel arrangements are discussed during the booking process.
Can Fidel customise his talk for our specific audience?
Absolutely. Every keynote is customised based on your audience, industry, and event goals. Fidel does not deliver copy-paste talks.
